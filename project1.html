Kashmir Eatrey surving tasty and healthy food since 1980

I set up a resturant in early 80's and have been surving the people of kashmir with tea , coffee , mixed food and a number of dishes , commonly eaten in kashmir . This resturant is at Nishat, almost at the bank of the Dal lake. The tea, coffee and biryani etc. are availabe in the resturant on reasonable rates and this is what fetches the resturant lot of people who routinely overcrowd the resturant.

The food that is availabe in the resturant include biryani, somasa and Chutni chicken and naan, barbeque and chutni, patato chips, meat balls,omelate and lastly fried rice. There is an addition of variety of ice cream in summer and delicious harisa in winter.
